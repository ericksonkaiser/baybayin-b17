B17+ Phonetic Rules

1

Kung ano ang bigkas: Jerome = "Dye-rom" → ᜇ᜔ᜌᜒᜇᜓᜋ᜔, hindi "dya-e-ro-me"

2

Foreign Letters: J→dy (dyey), C(ce/ci)→s, C(ca/co/cu)→k, F→p, V→b, X→ks, Z→s, Q→kw

3

Silent Letters: Drop silent 'e' sa dulo. Jerome → Dyerom, name → neym

4

Particles: "ng" = ᜅ᜔, "nang" = ᜈᜅ᜔, "mga" = ᜋᜅ
